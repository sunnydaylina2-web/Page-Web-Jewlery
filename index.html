About Aurum

Aurum Jewelry is a small studio dedicated to handcrafted pieces made in limited batches. Each design blends minimalist lines with warm gold accents, created to feel timeless from the first wear to the hundredth.

Our philosophy is simple: thoughtful materials, careful finishing, and jewelry that complements your everyday story.
